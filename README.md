## 📌 Project Overview

RetailPulse KSA is a Business Intelligence project that analyzes fashion retail sales performance across Saudi Arabia from 2020 to 2025.

The dashboard transforms raw business data into actionable insights, helping stakeholders identify growth opportunities, monitor profitability, optimize inventory, and improve strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

✔ Analyze sales performance across Saudi cities

✔ Identify the most profitable products and brands

✔ Monitor revenue, profit, and inventory KPIs

✔ Detect operational inefficiencies and business risks

✔ Support data-driven decision-making

## 🚨 Data Quality Issues Identified

### 1️⃣ Revenue Inconsistency

Net Revenue exceeds Gross Revenue, suggesting potential calculation or transformation issues.

### 2️⃣ Missing Returns Data

Returned products are not included in the dataset.

### 3️⃣ Fraud Detection Gap

No indicators for:

* Fraudulent Transactions
* Chargebacks
* Refund Analysis
* Risk Scoring

### 4️⃣ Missing Customer Analytics

Customer demographics and loyalty information are unavailable.

### 5️⃣ Sales Channel Visibility

No distinction between:

* Online Sales
* Mobile App Sales
* Physical Store Sales
